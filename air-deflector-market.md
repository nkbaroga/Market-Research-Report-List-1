<p><p>The air deflector market comprises various manufacturing types, including casting, molding, and vacuum forming. Casting involves pouring liquid material into molds to create detailed shapes, suitable for customized designs. Molding utilizes pre-formed molds to shape materials, often enhancing production efficiency and consistency. Vacuum forming involves heating a plastic sheet and using vacuum pressure to shape it over a mold, allowing for lightweight, intricate designs. Each method caters to distinct needs in durability, design complexity, and production scale, driving market diversity.</p></p>
