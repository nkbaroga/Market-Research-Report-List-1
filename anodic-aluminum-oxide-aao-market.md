The AAO market comprises two main types: Nanoporous Anodic Aluminum Oxide, characterized by pore sizes in the nanometer range, ideal for applications in nanotechnology and sensor fabrication; and Porous Anodic Aluminum Oxide, which features larger pore sizes and is commonly used in filtration, catalysis, and as templates for nanomaterial synthesis. Both types offer diverse industrial applications due to their tunable properties.</p></p>
