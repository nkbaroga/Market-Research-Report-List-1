<p><p>The Arc Sensor market encompasses various types, notably Point Sensors and Fiber Optic Sensors. Point Sensors are compact devices that detect arcs or electrical discharges at specific locations, providing quick and precise measurements. In contrast, Fiber Optic Sensors utilize light transmission through fiber optics to detect arcs over longer distances, offering high sensitivity and immunity to electromagnetic interference. Both types are essential for applications in power systems, ensuring safety and reliability in electrical installations and preventing equipment damage.</p></p>
