<p><p>The Artificial Intelligence Edge Device Market comprises three primary types: CPUs, GPUs, and ASICs. CPUs (Central Processing Units) are versatile processors suitable for general tasks but may lack efficiency in specific AI workloads. GPUs (Graphics Processing Units) excel in handling parallel processing, making them ideal for complex AI computations. ASICs (Application-Specific Integrated Circuits) are highly specialized chips designed for specific algorithms, offering superior performance and energy efficiency but with less flexibility. Together, these technologies enhance AI capabilities at the edge.</p></p>
