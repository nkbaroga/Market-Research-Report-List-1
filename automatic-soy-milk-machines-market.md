<p><p>The automatic soy milk machines market is segmented into two main types: with mesh and without mesh. Machines with mesh utilize a filtration system to separate soy milk from the soybean residue, ensuring a smooth consistency and reducing sediment. In contrast, machines without mesh employ a grinding and boiling process that mixes the soybeans entirely, resulting in a thicker, creamier product devoid of additional straining. Each type caters to different consumer preferences for texture and ease of use, influencing market dynamics.</p></p>
