<p><p>The automotive light bulb market consists of three primary types: halogen, xenon, and LED. Halogen bulbs are traditional, providing bright, white light at an affordable price but with shorter lifespans. Xenon, or HID bulbs, offer greater brightness and efficiency, producing a bluish-white light, making them popular in higher-end vehicles. LED bulbs are energy-efficient and have longer lifespans, providing versatile design options and superior visibility. Each type caters to different consumer preferences, vehicle models, and performance requirements in the automotive industry.</p></p>
