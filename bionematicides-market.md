<p><p>The **Bionematicides Market** includes **Microbials** and **Biochemicals**. **Microbial bionematicides** utilize beneficial microorganisms like bacteria, fungi, or nematodes to control plant-parasitic nematodes by disrupting their lifecycle or outcompeting them. **Biochemical bionematicides** are derived from natural compounds, such as plant extracts or organic substances, that interfere with nematode behavior or physiology.
