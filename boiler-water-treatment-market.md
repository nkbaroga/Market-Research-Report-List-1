<p><p>The boiler water treatment market includes essential chemical categories that ensure efficient operation and longevity of boiler systems. Corrosion inhibitors protect metal surfaces from oxidative damage, preventing leaks and failures. Scale inhibitors minimize mineral deposition, which can hinder heat transfer and efficiency. Oxygen scavengers eliminate dissolved oxygen in water, reducing the risk of corrosion significantly. Together, these treatments enhance boiler performance, reduce maintenance costs, and extend equipment lifespan, making them crucial for industries relying on steam generation.</p></p>
