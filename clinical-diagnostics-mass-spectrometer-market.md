<p><p>The Clinical Diagnostics Mass Spectrometer market primarily includes three types: LC-MS (Liquid Chromatography-Mass Spectrometry), GC-MS (Gas Chromatography-Mass Spectrometry), and Others. LC-MS is widely used for analyzing biomolecules and complex samples due to its sensitivity and versatility. GC-MS is favored for volatile and semi-volatile compounds, making it ideal for toxicology and environmental samples. The "Others" category encompasses advanced techniques like MALDI-TOF and ICP-MS, which cater to specific diagnostic applications, broadening the overall market scope in clinical diagnostics.</p></p>
