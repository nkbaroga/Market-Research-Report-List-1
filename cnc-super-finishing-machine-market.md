<p><strong>The CNC Super-Finishing Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vertical Super-Finishing Machine</li><li>Horizontal Super-Finishing Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The CNC super-finishing machine market encompasses two primary types: vertical and horizontal super-finishing machines. Vertical super-finishing machines are designed for processing components with a vertically oriented spindle, which allows for efficient use of gravity in finishing applications, ideal for cylindrical or complex-shaped workpieces. In contrast, horizontal super-finishing machines feature a horizontally oriented spindle, providing greater stability and precision for larger components. Both types enhance surface quality, reduce friction, and improve wear resistance in various industrial applications.</p></p>
