<p><p>The **Coating Glass Market** includes **Heat Reflective Glass (Solar Control Glass)** and **Low-E (Low Emissivity) Glass**, both designed to enhance energy efficiency. Heat Reflective Glass reduces solar heat gain by reflecting sunlight, maintaining cooler interiors and reducing cooling costs. Low-E Glass minimizes heat transfer by reflecting infrared light while allowing visible light, improving insulation and reducing energy consumption for heating and cooling. Both types are widely used in residential, commercial, and automotive applications for sustainability and comfort.</p></p>
