<p><p>The commercial espresso machine market is divided into three main types: manual, semi-automatic, and fully automatic. Manual machines require baristas to control every aspect of the brewing process, offering greater skill and customization. Semi-automatic machines combine manual operation with automated temperature control, allowing for some convenience while still requiring user involvement. Fully automatic machines automate the entire brewing process, making them ideal for high-volume settings where consistency and speed are crucial, catering to diverse café needs.</p></p>
