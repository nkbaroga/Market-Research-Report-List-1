<p><p>The commercial HVAC chillers market comprises two main types: air-cooled and water-cooled chillers. Air-cooled chillers use ambient air to dissipate heat, making them suitable for smaller applications and easier to install. Water-cooled chillers, on the other hand, utilize water as a coolant and are generally more efficient for larger buildings, offering better performance and lower operating costs. The choice between these types depends on factors such as installation space, cooling capacity requirements, and environmental considerations.</p></p>
