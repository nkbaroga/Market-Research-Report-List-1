<p><p>The composite cardboard tubes market comprises two primary types: paperboard tubes and corrugated board tubes. Paperboard tubes are typically made from a single layer of thick paper that offers smooth surfaces, used for packaging smaller, lighter products. In contrast, corrugated board tubes consist of a layered structure providing enhanced strength and durability, suitable for heavier or bulkier items. Both types are essential in various applications, including shipping, storage, and display, catering to diverse industry needs in packaging solutions.</p></p>
