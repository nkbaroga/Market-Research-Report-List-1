<p><p>The cosmetic pigments and dyes market is divided into two main types: organic and inorganic. Organic pigments are derived from carbon-based compounds, offering vibrant colors and are often used in formulations for makeup and skincare products. In contrast, inorganic pigments are mineral-based, providing stability and excellent opacity, commonly found in sunscreens and foundations. Both types serve distinct purposes in cosmetics, catering to varying consumer preferences for safety, vibrancy, and longevity in cosmetic applications.</p></p>
