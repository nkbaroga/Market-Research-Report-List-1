<p><ul><li>EMC Radiation Test</li><li>EMC Immunity Test</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Coupling Decoupling Networks (CDNs) are essential in EMC radiation and immunity testing, facilitating accurate evaluations of electronic devices against electromagnetic interference. In EMC radiation tests, CDNs enable controlled coupling of signals into the device under test, assessing its emissions. Conversely, in EMC immunity tests, they decouple external interference, ensuring that the device can withstand environmental electromagnetic disturbances. The growing demand for compliance with international standards in multiple industries drives the CDN market, underscoring its critical role in ensuring device reliability and safety.</p></p>
