<p><p>Digital Subtraction Angiography (DSA) is an advanced imaging technique used primarily in the medical field for visualizing blood vessels. It involves the subtraction of pre-contrast images from post-contrast images, enhancing visibility of vascular structures while minimizing the background anatomy. This method is integral for diagnosing and treating various vascular conditions by providing clear images of blood flow, enabling more precise interventions.</p><p>The Digital Subtraction Angiography (DSA) System Market is poised for significant growth, expected to witness a compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 11.1% during the forecast period. This surge is driven by factors such as increasing prevalence of cardiovascular diseases, advancements in imaging technology, and a growing elderly population that necessitates more vascular procedures. Additionally, the rise in minimally invasive surgeries and a focus on improving diagnostic accuracy further bolster market expansion. Emerging trends include the integration of AI and machine learning to enhance image quality and interpretation, as well as the development of portable DSA systems, which promote accessibility in diverse healthcare settings. These factors collectively contribute to the evolving landscape of the DSA market, highlighting its critical role in modern medical diagnostics and interventional procedures.</p></p>
