<p><p>The document scanner market comprises various technologies, including CCD (Charge-Coupled Device), CIS (Contact Image Sensor), and CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor). CCD scanners are known for high image quality and are often used in professional environments. CIS scanners are compact, cost-effective, and suitable for bulk scanning, making them popular for everyday use. CMOS technology offers energy efficiency and high-speed scanning, often found in portable and low-cost devices. Each type serves distinct needs based on image quality, speed, and application.</p></p>
