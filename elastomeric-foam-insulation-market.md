<p><p>Elastomeric foam insulation is a flexible, closed-cell material used for thermal and acoustic insulation. It comes in various types based on the polymer used. **NBR-based** foam, derived from nitrile rubber, offers excellent resistance to oils and fuels, making it ideal for industrial applications. **EPDM-based** foam, made from ethylene propylene diene monomer, excels in weather resistance and durability, suitable for outdoor use. **Chloroprene-based** foam, known for its fire resistance and flexibility, is often used in HVAC systems. **Others** include blends or specialty formulations tailored for specific performance needs.</p></p>
