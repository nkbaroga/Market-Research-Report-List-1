<p><strong>The Electric Instantaneous Water Heater Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Whole-House</li><li>Point-of-Use</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electric instantaneous water heater market is divided into two main types: whole-house and point-of-use. Whole-house heaters provide a continuous supply of hot water to multiple outlets simultaneously, making them ideal for larger homes. They are typically installed near the main water supply. In contrast, point-of-use heaters are compact units installed near specific faucets or appliances, delivering hot water on demand at that location. This type is more energy-efficient for small spaces or for supplementing existing systems.</p></p>
