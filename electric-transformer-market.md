<p><p>The electric transformer market comprises several types, each serving specific purposes. Isolation transformers provide electrical isolation and safety, reducing the risk of shock and interference. Autotransformers enhance efficiency by using a common winding for both primary and secondary circuits, often in voltage regulation applications. Toroidal transformers, with their donut-shaped core, offer lower electromagnetic interference and are compact, suitable for sensitive equipment. Other market types include three-phase transformers and step-up/step-down transformers, catering to diverse industrial and commercial needs.</p></p>
