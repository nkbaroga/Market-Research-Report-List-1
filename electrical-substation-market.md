<p><p>Electrical substations play a critical role in power distribution and management. The market is primarily categorized into Air-Insulated Substations (AIS), Gas-Insulated Substations (GIS), and other types. AIS substations utilize air as the insulating medium, making them cost-effective but requiring more space. GIS substations, on the other hand, use gas for insulation, offering a compact design with better performance in limited spaces and extreme environments. Other types may include mobile substations or hybrid designs, catering to specific operational needs or constraints.</p></p>
