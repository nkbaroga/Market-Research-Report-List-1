<p><p>Emulsion Pressure Sensitive Adhesives (PSAs) are water-based adhesives that form a bond when light pressure is applied. They are widely used in industries such as packaging, automotive, healthcare, and electronics due to their eco-friendly nature, ease of application, and strong bonding properties. These adhesives are composed of polymers dispersed in water, making them non-toxic and easy to handle compared to solvent-based alternatives.</p><p>The Emulsion Pressure Sensitive Adhesives Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10.8% during the forecast period. This growth is driven by increasing demand for sustainable and low-VOC (volatile organic compound) adhesives, particularly in packaging and labeling applications. The rise in e-commerce and the need for efficient packaging solutions further boost market expansion. Additionally, advancements in adhesive technologies, such as improved temperature resistance and durability, are enhancing their applicability in diverse industries.</p><p>Key trends in the market include the development of bio-based emulsion PSAs to meet environmental regulations and consumer preferences for green products.
