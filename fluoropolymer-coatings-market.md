<p><p>The fluoropolymer coatings market encompasses various types, each with distinct properties and applications. PTFE coatings offer exceptional non-stick and chemical resistance, ideal for cookware and industrial equipment. PVDF coatings provide durability and weather resistance, commonly used in architectural finishes. FEP coatings combine flexibility with high-temperature stability, suitable for electrical insulation and wiring. ETFE coatings are known for their lightweight and high corrosion resistance, making them popular in aerospace and architectural applications. Each type serves unique industries and needs.</p></p>
