<p><strong>The Food Extrusion Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-screw Extruder</li><li>Twin-screw Extruder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The food extrusion equipment market primarily features two types of extruders: single-screw and twin-screw extruders. Single-screw extruders are more straightforward, utilizing a single rotating screw to process materials, making them cost-effective for simpler applications. In contrast, twin-screw extruders have two intermeshing screws, allowing for better mixing, higher shear, and greater control over the extrusion process. This makes them suitable for more complex formulations and product designs, catering to diverse food processing needs. Both types play essential roles in manufacturing snacks, cereals, and other processed foods.</p></p>
