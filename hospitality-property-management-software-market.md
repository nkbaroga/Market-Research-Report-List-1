<p><ul><li>On-Premise</li><li>Cloud-Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hospitality Property Management Software (PMS) is classified into two main market types: on-premise and cloud-based solutions. On-premise software is installed locally on the hotel’s servers, offering greater control and security but requiring significant upfront investment and maintenance. In contrast, cloud-based PMS allows hotels to access the system via the internet, promoting flexibility, scalability, and lower initial costs. Cloud solutions are often easier to update and integrate with other systems, making them increasingly popular among modern hospitality businesses.</p></p>
