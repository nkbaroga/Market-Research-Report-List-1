<p><p>The industrial robot speed reducer market comprises three primary types: RV reducers, harmonic reducers, and planetary speed reducers. RV reducers are known for their compact design and high torque-to-weight ratio, making them suitable for robotic arms. Harmonic reducers offer exceptional precision and flexibility, ideal for applications requiring exact movements. Planetary speed reducers feature a system of gears that distribute load evenly, providing durability and efficiency, making them popular in various automation applications. Together, they enhance the performance and reliability of industrial robots.</p></p>
