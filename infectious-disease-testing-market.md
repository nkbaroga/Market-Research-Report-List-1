<p><p>The infectious disease testing market encompasses various types of diagnostic methods. Immunodiagnostics utilize antibodies for rapid detection, while clinical microbiology involves culturing pathogens. PCR (Polymerase Chain Reaction) amplifies genetic material for precise identification, and INAAT (Isothermal Nucleic Acid Amplification Testing) offers rapid results without complex equipment. DNA sequencing and NGS (Next-Generation Sequencing) provide comprehensive genomic analysis, while DNA microarrays enable simultaneous detection of multiple pathogens. Other market segments include emerging technologies and point-of-care testing solutions, enhancing diagnostic capabilities.</p></p>
