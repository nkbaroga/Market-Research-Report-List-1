<p><ul><li>Nonlinear Photoelectric Coupler</li><li>Linear Photoelectric Coupler</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Integrated Circuit Optical Couplers market is categorized primarily into nonlinear and linear photoelectric couplers. Nonlinear photoelectric couplers enable signal processing through nonlinear optical effects, making them suitable for applications with high-frequency or complex signals. In contrast, linear photoelectric couplers function based on linear relationships, providing stable and predictable performance for applications requiring precise signal transmission. Both types play crucial roles in communication systems and signal management, facilitating efficient data transfer in various electronic devices.</p></p>
