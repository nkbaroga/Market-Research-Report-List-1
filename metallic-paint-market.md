<p><strong>The Metallic Paint Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solvent Based</li><li>Water Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The metallic paint market is categorized into solvent-based and water-based types. Solvent-based metallic paints use organic solvents, offering a rich and durable finish, ideal for outdoor applications and high-traffic areas. They provide excellent adhesion and durability but can have higher VOCs. In contrast, water-based metallic paints utilize water as a primary solvent, making them more environmentally friendly with lower emissions. They dry quickly and are easier to clean, making them suitable for indoor applications, though they may offer slightly less durability.</p></p>
