<p><ul><li>Singlemode Microplate Luminometer</li><li>Multimode Microplate Luminometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The microplate luminometer market encompasses two primary types: single-mode and multimode microplate luminometers. Single-mode microplate luminometers are designed specifically for luminometric assays, providing high sensitivity for applications like reporter gene assays and ATP detection. In contrast, multimode microplate luminometers offer versatility by supporting multiple detection modes, including fluorescence, absorbance, and luminescence, making them suitable for a broader range of applications. This flexibility allows researchers to optimize their experiments and increase throughput in various laboratory settings.</p></p>
