<p><p>Mycology immunoassay testing encompasses various methods for detecting fungal infections. ELISA (Enzyme-Linked Immunosorbent Assay) is widely used for quantifying antibodies or antigens. Rapid tests provide quick results for fungal pathogens, enhancing diagnosis speed. ELISPOT detects specific immune responses, useful in assessing infection status. Polymerase Chain Reaction (PCR) amplifies fungal DNA, allowing for sensitive and specific identification of pathogens. Together, these techniques cater to diverse clinical needs in fungal diagnostics and treatment monitoring.</p></p>
