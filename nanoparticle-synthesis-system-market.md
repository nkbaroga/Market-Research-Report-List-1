<p><p>The nanoparticle synthesis system market includes various techniques, primarily colloidal nanoparticle synthesis and spark ablation nanoparticle synthesis. Colloidal synthesis involves the chemical reduction of precursors in a solution to produce nanoparticles with controlled size and shape, making it suitable for various applications like drug delivery and catalysis. Spark ablation synthesis, on the other hand, utilizes electric sparks to vaporize materials, generating nanoparticles through rapid cooling. This method allows for the production of high-purity nanoparticles with distinct properties, ideal for advanced materials and coatings.</p></p>
