Low-heat nonfat dried milk retains more nutrients and is ideal for baking and confectionery applications. Medium-heat is processed at higher temperatures, resulting in enhanced solubility and flavor, suitable for dairy products and beverages. High-heat nonfat dried milk undergoes intense heat treatment, making it suitable for processed foods where longer shelf life and stability are required. Each type serves distinct industrial applications.</p></p>
