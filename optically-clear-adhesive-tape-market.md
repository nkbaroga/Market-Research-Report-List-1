<p><strong>The Optically Clear Adhesive Tape Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Liquid based</li><li>Non-liquid tape</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Optically Clear Adhesive (OCA) tapes are used in displays and touchscreens for bonding layers without compromising clarity. The market is divided into liquid-based and non-liquid (solid) tape types. Liquid-based OCA tapes are applied as a fluid, curing to form a transparent bond, ideal for complex shapes and flexible displays. Non-liquid OCA tapes are pre-cut, pressure-sensitive films, offering ease of application and consistent thickness, commonly used in smartphones and tablets. Both types ensure optical clarity, durability, and strong adhesion for high-performance display applications.</p></p>
