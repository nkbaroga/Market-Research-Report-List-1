<p><p>The paper bed filter market is divided into two main types: without magnetic and magnetic filters. Without magnetic filters utilize standard paper media to capture particles from liquids or gases, making them ideal for applications where magnets are not viable. Magnetic filters, on the other hand, are enhanced with magnetic elements that attract ferrous particles, improving filtration efficiency in environments where metal contaminants are present. Both types serve diverse industries, ensuring effective filtration solutions tailored to specific operational needs.</p></p>
