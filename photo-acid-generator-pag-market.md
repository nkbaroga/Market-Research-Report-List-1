<p><p>Photo Acid Generators (PAGs) are crucial components in photoresist formulations used in photolithography, particularly in semiconductor manufacturing. The market for PAGs can be divided into two main types: ionic and non-ionic. Ionic PAGs produce a strong acid when exposed to light, facilitating high-resolution patterning, while non-ionic PAGs release a weaker acid, offering benefits like better thermal stability and reduced metal contamination. Each type serves specific application needs, influencing their demand in various industries, including electronics and coatings.</p></p>
