<p><p>Power Line Communication (PLC) markets are generally categorized into narrowband and broadband segments. Narrowband PLC operates over limited frequency ranges, supporting applications like smart metering and remote monitoring, characterized by lower data rates but long-range capabilities. In contrast, broadband PLC provides higher data transfers, enabling internet access and multimedia applications, suitable for residential and commercial use. These segments cater to different demands, with narrowband focusing on utility and automation, while broadband emphasizes connectivity and high-speed data transmission.</p></p>
