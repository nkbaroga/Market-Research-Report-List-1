<p><strong>The qPCR Reagents Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dye-based Reagents</li><li>Probe & Primer-based Reagents</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The qPCR reagents market is categorized into dye-based reagents and probe & primer-based reagents. Dye-based reagents, such as SYBR Green, bind to double-stranded DNA, enabling fluorescence detection during amplification. They are simpler and cost-effective but less specific. On the other hand, probe & primer-based reagents utilize specific probes, such as TaqMan or molecular beacons, that provide higher specificity and quantification accuracy. Their use is critical in applications requiring precise measurement of target genes or pathogens.</p></p>
