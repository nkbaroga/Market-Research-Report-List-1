<p><p>The quadrant detector market can be categorized into two main types: 2-quadrant detectors and 4-quadrant detectors. Two-quadrant detectors measure signals in two quadrants of a system, primarily focusing on positive and negative values of a single input. In contrast, four-quadrant detectors can handle signals across all four quadrants, providing comprehensive measurement of both amplitude and phase information. These detectors are widely used in applications such as optical sensing, telecommunications, and industrial automation, catering to diverse technological needs.</p></p>
