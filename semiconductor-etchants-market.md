<p><strong>The Semiconductor Etchants Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wet Etching Agent</li><li>Dry Etching Agent</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconductor etchants market comprises two primary types: wet etching agents and dry etching agents. Wet etching involves using liquid chemicals to remove material from semiconductor surfaces, allowing for precise control over the etching process in applications like patterning and cleaning. Conversely, dry etching employs gases or plasmas to achieve material removal, enabling greater accuracy at smaller scales, which is essential for modern semiconductor manufacturing. Both types play critical roles in producing advanced electronic components.</p></p>
