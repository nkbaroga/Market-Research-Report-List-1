<p><strong>The Semiconductor Packaging and Test Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wafer Probe Station</li><li>Die Bonder</li><li>Dicing Machine</li><li>Test handler</li><li>Sorter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconductor packaging and test equipment market includes various essential types: </p><p>1. **Wafer Probe Station**: Used for testing semiconductor wafers before dicing, ensuring chip functionality.</p><p>2. **Die Bonder**: Responsible for attaching the die to the substrate or package, critical for device assembly.</p><p>3. **Dicing Machine**: Cuts the wafer into individual dies, essential for creating separate chips.</p><p>4. **Test Handler**: Automates the testing process of packaged semiconductors, improving efficiency.</p><p>5. **Sorter**: Categorizes and sorts tested chips based on their performance, enhancing production workflow.</p></p>
