<p><ul><li>Lead Free Solder Baths</li><li>Ordinary Solder Baths</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The solder baths market comprises two main types: lead-free solder baths and ordinary solder baths. Lead-free solder baths utilize environmentally friendly materials, typically composed of tin, silver, and copper, making them suitable for industries seeking compliance with regulations against lead usage. Ordinary solder baths, on the other hand, traditionally contain lead-based alloys, offering effective thermal conductivity and lower costs but raising health and environmental concerns. As regulations tighten, a shift toward lead-free options is increasingly evident across sectors.</p></p>
