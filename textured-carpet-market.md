<p><p>The textured carpet market features two main types: cut pile and loop pile carpets. Cut pile textured carpets are created by cutting the yarn loops, resulting in a plush, soft surface with a luxurious feel, ideal for residential spaces. Loop pile textured carpets, on the other hand, maintain the loops intact, offering durability and a more casual appearance; they are suitable for high-traffic areas. Both types enhance aesthetic appeal and comfort, catering to diverse consumer preferences in flooring options.</p></p>
