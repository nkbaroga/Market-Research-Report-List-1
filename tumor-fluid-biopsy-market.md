<p><p>The tumor fluid biopsy market encompasses various detection methods: ctDNA (circulating tumor DNA), CTC (circulating tumor cells), and other market segments. ctDNA detection involves analyzing fragments of tumor DNA found in blood or other body fluids, providing insights into genetic mutations and tumor dynamics. CTC detection focuses on identifying and characterizing live tumor cells circulating in the bloodstream, which aids in assessing metastasis. The "Other" category includes emerging technologies like exosomes and protein biomarkers, enriching the market's diagnostic capabilities.</p></p>
