<p><p>The vehicle diagnostic scan tool market includes various types, each serving specific purposes. Scanners are advanced tools for deep diagnostics and real-time data monitoring. Code readers are simpler devices that retrieve fault codes from the vehicle's computer. TPMS tools specialize in monitoring tire pressure and sensors. Digital pressure testers assess fluid pressures in various systems, while battery analyzers evaluate battery health and performance. Together, these tools enhance vehicle maintenance and troubleshooting efficiency for both professionals and DIY enthusiasts.</p></p>
