<p><strong>The Voice over LTE Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Frequency Division Duplexing (FDD)</li><li>Time Division Duplexing (TDD)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Voice over LTE (VoLTE) market is categorized into two primary types based on duplexing methods: Frequency Division Duplexing (FDD) and Time Division Duplexing (TDD). FDD uses separate frequency bands for uplink and downlink, enabling simultaneous voice and data transmission, which enhances call quality and overall network efficiency. Conversely, TDD employs a shared frequency for both uplink and downlink, allocating time slots for each, optimizing spectrum use but potentially impacting real-time communication. Both methods serve distinct network requirements and user experiences.</p></p>
