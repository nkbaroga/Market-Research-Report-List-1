<p><p>The Wafer Back Grinding Tapes market is segmented into two main types: UV Type and Non-UV Type. UV Type tapes utilize ultraviolet light to cure adhesive, offering advantages such as improved adhesion and easier removal after processing. Non-UV Type tapes, on the other hand, rely on thermal or other curing methods, making them suitable for specific applications where UV exposure is not feasible. Both types serve critical roles in semiconductor manufacturing, ensuring efficient and precise back grinding processes.</p></p>
