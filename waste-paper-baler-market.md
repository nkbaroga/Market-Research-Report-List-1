<p><ul><li>Horizontal</li><li>Vertical</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The waste paper baler market is primarily categorized into horizontal and vertical balers. Horizontal balers are designed for high-volume operations, efficiently compressing material into dense bales for easy handling and transport. They require more floor space but offer faster cycle times. In contrast, vertical balers occupy less space and are suitable for smaller operations, producing bales in a vertical format. While they are generally less productive than horizontal models, they are cost-effective for businesses with lower waste volumes.</p></p>
