<p><p>Wavelength Division Multiplexer (WDM) technology enables multiple data signals to be transmitted simultaneously over a single optical fiber by assigning different wavelengths to each signal. The market is primarily divided into Coarse Wavelength Division Multiplexer (CWDM) and Dense Wavelength Division Multiplexer (DWDM). CWDM utilizes wider channel spacing and is suitable for shorter distance applications, while DWDM offers tighter channel spacing for higher capacity and longer distances, making it ideal for long-haul telecommunications and high-bandwidth applications.</p></p>
